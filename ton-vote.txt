HOOKAH GLOBAL SYSTEM (HOK) is the world's first DAO that merges hookah culture with Web3 economy on the TON blockchain. Our mission is to build a global community for hookah enthusiasts powered by blockchain transparency and shared rewards. HOK aims to launch a TIP-3 standard token on TON, create a verified DAO on ton.vote, and reward community members while expanding hookah culture worldwide. The token HOK (HOOKAH GLOBAL SYSTEM) will power governance, rewards, and ecosystem growth across the global hookah community.
